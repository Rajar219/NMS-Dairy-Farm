The Morning Run

From udder to your doorstep before the city wakes up

Here's what happens to your milk every single morning.

4:30 AM

Milking

Our herd is milked fresh every morning — cow and buffalo both, in one go.

5:15 AM

Filtered & Chilled

Passed through a clean filter — nothing added. Kept cool for the route.

5:45 AM

Packed

Sealed into cans by order — cow and buffalo separate, labelled for your route.

6 – 8:30 AM

Delivered

At your door before breakfast — fresh from this morning's milking, every day.
